4x4 House

Exhibition Langen Foundation Home Biography

During his design on the 4x4 House, Ando wanted to design a house that wasn’t your conventional house that stood out but also retain its simplicity. From his work, alot of his signature deisgns are in play like the use of the smooth concrete texture and conrete color. The deisgn was also unarthodox as most houses didn’t follow the same shape like Ando’s 4x4 house. He design it in a way that would be symtrical when the 2 houses were stood together. The houses were also very simple as there were no intricate design aspects to the houses.

"I don't believe architecture has to speak too much. It should remain silent and let nature in the guise of sunlight and wind."

“To create architecture is to express characteristic aspects of the real world such as nature, history, tradition and society, in a spatial structure”
