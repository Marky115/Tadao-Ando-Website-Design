Exhibition 4x4 House Home Biography

In his deisgn process, Ando wanted to reflect the mood of calmness when he felt walking through his inspiring sties at naoshima museum in Japan. The structure of the building is combine with many natural elements to give a sense of stillness and calmness such as incoporating the cherry blossom trees with the water. He also wanted the building to be very clear and transparent. Natural lighting is greatly emphasize in the deisgn. He uses alot of glass surrounding the entire half of the building which lets alot of natural light in as well as he uses the color white. This help create an tranquality mood around the design of the building.

“The essence of Minimalism is simplicity, but simplicity without depth is merely cheap. It is not enough.”

“I hope to achieve simplicity, but I also hope to achieve depth. I believe it is important that architecture should be a space where you feel spiritually empowered.”
